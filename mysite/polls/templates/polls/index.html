Lime Stone

Limestone often contains variable amounts of silica in the form of chert and/or flint, as well as varying amounts of clay, silt and sand as disseminations, nodules, or layers within the rock.

Stand Stone

Sandstone is a sedimentary rock composed mainly of sand-size mineral or rock grains. Most sandstone is composed of quartz and/or feldspar because these are the most common minerals in the Earth's crust.

Marbles

Marble is a non foliated metamorphic rock. It is extensively used for sculpture, as a building material, and in many other applications. Marbles are capable of taking a high polish.

Marbles

Granites

Granite is a common occurring type of intrusive, felsic, igneous rock.Granite is nearly always massive, hard and tough, and therefore it has gained widespread use as a construction stone. The average density of granite is 2.75 g/cm3
